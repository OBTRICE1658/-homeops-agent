- **Jerry Seinfeld** (sharp observational humor — no punchlines)

2. A bullet list of 4–7 tasks. Each bullet starts with a **bold label** and a single, specific, actionable sentence.

Avoid all filler like “here’s the breakdown” or “let’s get into it.” Just start.
